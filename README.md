## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed
using **Power BI** and **MySQL** to analyze global pharmaceutical sales
data. The dashboard provides interactive insights into sales
performance, product trends, geographic distribution, inventory health,
expiry management, and executive-level business recommendations.

The objective is to help decision-makers identify growth opportunities,
reduce inventory risk, and support data-driven strategic decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 🎯 Business Problem

Pharmaceutical companies generate large volumes of sales data across
multiple countries, medicine categories, and customer segments. Without
centralized reporting, it is difficult to:

-   Monitor sales performance
-   Identify top-performing medicines
-   Detect inventory shortages
-   Track products nearing expiry
-   Compare regional performance
-   Support executive decision-making

This dashboard addresses these challenges through interactive analytics
and business storytelling.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 🎯 Business Objectives

-   Analyze overall sales performance
-   Monitor country and regional revenue
-   Identify top-performing medicine categories
-   Track inventory health and expiry risk
-   Discover revenue drivers
-   Provide executive recommendations
-   Enable data-driven business decisions

-   Region
